Voxobuild: Legacy Edition

Voxobuild: Legacy Edition is a free Minecraft clone created in 6 weeks. You can place blocks, break blocks, and that's really all, but there's mods, so you could probably make the game NOT boring if you know how to write C#

Credits: S. K - Lead Development F. M - Steel Texture

Controls:

  • WASD: Walk
  • Mouse: Look
  • Left Click: Break
  • Right Click: Place
  • Scroll: Select Blocks
  • Tab: Select Sand
  • M: Import ModScript (.dll format)
  • MOD TUTORIAL: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1-2E9V4qSNcz5AVyDVgmCRTDhp8kK8HDAMAsyUT4HG0Y/edit?usp=sharing
  • Don't know where to host your mods? Join our Discord server!
